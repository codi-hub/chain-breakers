Giving

Your support and contributions enable us to spread the message of hope and love. Thank you for your generosity.

Ways to Give

  • Online Giving: You can securely give online through our website.
  • In-Person Giving: Join us during our Sunday service to make your contribution in person.
  • Mail: You can also mail your donation to Chain Breaker Ministries, 123 Church Street, Cityville.

Why Give

Giving is an act of worship and a way to partner with us in our mission to make a positive impact in the community.
